He has fabulous expertise in lab animal handling and experimentation. His expertise includes biomaterials design in special relation to tissue engineering scaffolds, synthesis and biocompatibility evaluation, implant pathology and tissue response evaluation in vivo with expert opinion on conduct of further research and commercialization aspects. Research Design

Biomaterials Analysis

Biomaterials analysis in current context refers to physico chemical analysis of materials that are being tested to be used as biomaterials implant, tissue engineering scaffolds and artificial devices.

The testing techniques may be spectroscopic (Various types of UV, Vis, NIR, FTIR, XRD, NMR, Atomic Absorption spectra etc.), Thermal (TGA, DSC, DMA etc.), Mechanical (Compresssive, Shear, Tensile, Flexural strength etc), Imaging (SEM, TEM, Steriomicroscopy, Fluorescence imaging, Optical profilometry) and many more techniques.

The highlight of our service is the optimised testing protocols designed specifically for biomaterials, which is much different from norma l materials testing used elsewhere.

Cell culture analysis

We offer cell culture analyses for biomaterials, phytochemicals and formulations.
Cell culture studies of biomaterials need a lot of expertise and material handling capabilities. We seamlessly integrate all these for our clients. We offer Adhesion analysis, Viability analysis, Proliferation analysis, Various enzyme activity studies etc. for biomaterisls.
For tisssue engineering scaffolds, we provide various additional analysis and services based upon the scaffold.
In cases of drugs, phytochemicals and formuations we offer cell culture analysis according to standard protocols.

Lab Animal Testing

Laboratory animal experimentation has always been a dream for many academic researchers. In the industrial perspective, there has always been a need for expertise on animal study design, execution and reporting according to GLP standards. We offer such services in various modes.
We have associations with CPCSEA approved labs to execute the research. We possess expertise to perform the study, hence can come to the place of your convinience, where there is approved facility, to perform the study for you.

Statistical Services

Statistics is an indisputable partner in research. When it come to biological research, performing statistical analysis is quite challenging, especially when it comes to arriving at the inference of the study. This area in research methodology is often filled with confusions and difficulties to understand for a biologist. We are here to simplify the entire process.
We integrate various experts and produce a complete report with your valuable data.
We also handle statistical data of surveys, epidemiology, meta analysis and procrustus analysis.
