About Us

RNR Research Services is a team of research people committed to provide cutting edge consultancy services to academic and industrial researchers. We are located in chennai. As a contract research provider and research report generators, we work in the field of biomaterials science and biomedical engineering. We tend to provide cost efficient and superior-quality solutions to academic and industrial research projects. Our services range from study design, statistics, execution of study with report along with the results to language and grammar services. We specialize in the field of biomaterials science and our expertise include all specialities involved, viz. Synthesis/Preparation of materials, Characterization, pre-clinical analysis, in vitro cell culture testing, in vivo testing and reporting. We also provide feasibility analysis of your study including latest techniques and publishability. For industries, we also offer consultation on product development and preclinical evaluation.

About the director

Dr. R. Narasimha Raghavan, BDS., MSc., PhD,
The Director,
Dental Surgeon and Biomaterials Scientist.

Dr. Raghavan is dental surgeon cum biomaterials scientist. He has a unique academic qualification. His professional education began with a graduation in dentistry with awards from professional societies for his academic capabilities. His interest towards research drove him to take up masters in biomaterials science from a premier university in Chennai, where he received rigorous training in materials science. He was selected for the prestigious INSPIRE Fellowship from Department of Science and Technology, Govt of India, (available only for university first rankers), which enabled him to do the doctoral programme, under the University of Madras in CSIR-Central Leather Research Institute.

His academic qualification is a unique blend of clinical knowledge coupled with materials design and evaluation for tissue engineering applications, well suited to help the graduate and postgraduate students to achieve productivity in biomedical research in their respective levels. He has fabulous expertise in lab animal handling and experimentation. His expertise includes biomaterials design in special relation to tissue engineering scaffolds, synthesis and biocompatibility evaluation, implant pathology and tissue response evaluation in vivo with expert opinion on conduct of further research and commercialization aspects. Further he has aptitude for development of novel products for clinical needs at low cost. We has a well knit professional network to get reasearch done on time, with cost effectiveness and great quality.
